  1. How did you get my CC Username and Password?
    We did not!
    Everytime you login, your username and password are checked against IITK CC servers, if they pass you are assumed a legitimate user.
  2. Will anyone be able to see my choices?
    Nobody except you can see your selections, not even the person you picked.
  3. Will you be able to see my choices?
    No!
    All your data is sent to the server in an encrypted form, which can only be decrypted by your browser.
  4. What happens on 14th feb?
    If you select someone and that person selects you too, we will reveal to both of you that you picked each other.
    But, if its just a one sided affair from you we would not notify him/her.
